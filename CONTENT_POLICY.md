# JumpAround — what belongs here

JumpAround is for the weird, cool, and useful stuff that got buried when the web got boring. One test: **will the person who lands here be glad they did?**

Made something yourself? Share it — your site, blog, art, game, little tool. That's the good stuff.

Work somewhere with more than 20 people? Sit this one out. JumpAround is for individuals and small crews, not marketing departments.

No ads. No storefronts, pricing pages, affiliate links, SEO sludge, crypto, or "free" tools that just want your email. If a page is trying to sell you something or grab your info, it's an ad — and it doesn't belong.

Break these and your page gets pulled. Do it again and you're banned.
